Our Programs


Sustainable Agriculture


We provide training and resources to help farmers adopt sustainable, environmentally friendly and economically viable practices such as crop rotation, organic farming, and water conservation.

We train and support small-holding farmers in sustainable livestock management practices such as animal health, breeding, and feed management.



Rural Development


We work with rural communities to identify their development needs and customize programs to address challenges specific to their respective communities. We facilitate the empowerment of these communities by promoting rural entrepreneurship and providing access to markets.



Natural Habitat Conservation


We are committed to protecting natural habitats, including forests, wetlands, and wildlife reserves. We collaborate with local communities and local authorities to develop sustainable strategies for conservation, such as ecotourism and community-based conservation initiatives.



Clean Energy Initiatives


Our clean energy initiatives include the promotion of solar and wind power to reduce our dependence on fossil fuels. We work with government agencies, businesses, and individuals to promote renewable energy adoption and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.



Environmental Education


Education is a key catalyst in promoting environmental awareness and encouraging climate action. Our programs in schools and colleges encourage sustainable practices from an early age. We facilitate the creation of eco-clubs and conduct workshops on sustainability, cultivation, and biodiversity.

Our “Adopt a Plant” activity encourages students to take care of a plant and report to the club. At Krishi Bharat, we work closely with students, teachers, and management to build a culture of sustainability in educational institutions.



Ecological Conservation


We have implemented several initiatives in rainwater harvesting, lake rejuvenation, afforestation, conceptual indoor and outdoor gardens, sanitation, and infrastructure development.
